Welcome

Welcome to the Invite-o-Matic! We're going to create an invitation you can forward to your son's sports team to invite them to an upcoming Cub Scout Event!

This is an open-source project on GitHub. If you'd like to help make this better, click the link in the header, and jump in to contribute!

Step 1: Choose what type of invitation you would like to create.

Step 2: Fill in your information.

Note: This information stays private. We aren't tracking you, and we don't even have a way to record what you've entered here.

What is your name?
What is your Scout's name?
What is your Scout's Pack number?
What is/was your Scout's team name?
When did your Scout play with this team?

Subject

Cub Scout Family Fun Day, Sunday 9/21 from 1-4pm @ Veteran’s Park Broomall

Body

Hello Terrapins!

My son Teddy enjoyed his time playing with you this past spring, and I wanted to share an activity that he enjoys with all of you: Cub Scouts!

The Cub Scouts are holding a Family Fun Day in Veteran’s Park in Broomall on Sunday, September 21 from 1-4pm. It’s a day where you can enjoy the Cub Scout experience and learn about what it is we do. We’ll have games, crafts, challenge courses and even a chance for everyone to learn some new skills. Admission is Free and everyone is welcome.

I will be there with Teddy at the Pack 151 & 468 booth. I hope to see you there!

If you have any questions, please reach out to me, and I’ll do my best to answer them.

Yours in Scouting,

Chris Pilko

P.S.: If you aren’t interested, I’m very sorry for bothering you. This is the only email I’m sending you about this. I did not pass your name on to anyone else, and you aren’t on any list.
